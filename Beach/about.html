Tikling Island is located in Matnog, Sorsogon, along with other equally beautiful islands and beaches including the relatively more popular Subic Beach. Tikling Island is about 30 minutes ride by outrigger boat from Matnog Port and about 10 minutes away from Subic Beach. The island is unique in its own way: its sand is pink instead of the usual white and brown, it is covered with a vast vegetation that stretches wherever the sand does, shallow and very clear waters as far as 50 meters from the shore, no signs of human intervention except for the nipa huts of the caretakers, ambiance/mood remains quiet and tranquil despite only a few minutes away from civilization. Typhoon Glenda just recently passed through most of Bicol when we visited the island. On the way, we have seen several trees and cottages severely damaged by the storm. It will definitely restore its beauty in due time so visitors just have to wait. Despite the damage, the island remains picturesque with its pink sand, blue green waters and lush greeneries.
