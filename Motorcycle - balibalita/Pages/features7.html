Yamaha NMAX 125 Features

Start & Stop

By riding such an economical scooter you're already helping lessen your carbon footprint, and to further reduce urban pollution and minimize fuel consumption the NMAX is equipped with a Start & Stop system that cuts in when you come to a stop at traffic lights or in congested streets. When the lights go green just releas the brakes and twist the throttle and the EUROS engine starts immediately.
